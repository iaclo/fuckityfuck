What Are Extended Auto Warranty Coverage Plans?

An extended warranty for your car is insurance that covers repairs or replacements to a vehicle that has not yet been covered by the manufacturer's warranty.

The policy generally lasts for a specific number of year and covers a wide variety of potential problems.

An extended car warranty, which is an agreement between the car owners and a warranty company, promises to cover some of costs associated with replacing or repairing car parts after the manufacturer's warranty expires.

Items that are covered by an extended warranty on a car include cars, appliances, and pre-owned goods.

An extended car warranty serves to enhance the factory warranty.

This means that it kicks into effect after the standard manufacturer warranty has expired.

Extended warranties usually cover additional expenses not covered by the factory warranty, such as brake pads and batteries.
